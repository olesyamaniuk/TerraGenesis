TerraGenesis: Landfall

Shape New Worlds. Build Civilizations. Explore the Unknown in TerraGenesis: Landfall!

About the Game

TerraGenesis: Landfall is a groundbreaking strategy and simulation game where you take control of an untouched planet and transform it into a thriving ecosystem. From barren wastelands to lush landscapes, you’ll guide the process of terraforming, develop sustainable colonies, and balance ecological harmony with technological advancements.

  • Terraform Planets: Utilize cutting-edge technology to change planetary environments, turning deserts into fertile land, creating oceans, and fostering new life.

  • Build Advanced Colonies: Establish futuristic settlements, design infrastructure, and manage resources to ensure the survival and growth of your population.

  • Discover New Worlds: Explore diverse planets with unique challenges, climates, and terrain. Each world presents its own opportunities and dangers.

  • Shape Your Civilization: Make strategic decisions that affect the development of society, from political systems to scientific advancements and ethical choices.

  • Balance Nature and Progress: Maintain ecological balance while advancing technology. Protect biodiversity or risk catastrophic consequences for your planet.

What Makes It Unique?

TerraGenesis: Landfall stands apart from other simulation and strategy games by offering a deeply immersive experience rooted in real-world science and planetary ecology. Unlike traditional city-building games, this title challenges players to think beyond the immediate needs of a settlement, focusing on the long-term health and viability of an entire planet.

  • Science-Driven Gameplay: Based on principles of astrobiology and planetary science, the game provides an educational yet thrilling simulation of terraforming.

  • Dynamic Ecosystems: Each decision affects the planet’s climate, terrain, and biodiversity, creating a living, evolving world.

  • Diverse Planetary Environments: Players can choose from a variety of planets, each with its own unique challenges and possibilities.

  • Ethical Dilemmas: The game introduces moral decisions, such as balancing population growth with environmental preservation, adding depth to gameplay.
